What Authenticity Validator Academia (AVA) Does

AVA is a secure academic verification platform designed to eliminate fake documents, simplify admission processes, and ensure trust between colleges and students. Our system bridges institutions to verify academic records instantly and transparently.

1. Instant Document Verification

Colleges can upload and validate marksheets and certificates in seconds through secure matching using document numbers and issuance history.

2. Secure Inter-College Record Sharing

Institutions can verify documents issued by other colleges, ensuring authenticity during admissions, transfers, and postgraduate applications.

3. Student Profile & Document Vault

Each student has a centralized academic vault where all issued documents are stored digitally for future use.

4. Fraud Prevention & Transparency

AVA prevents misuse of forged documents by tracking the issuing college, issue date, and verifying against original records.

How AVA Works

Authenticity Validator Academia (AVA) is designed to help colleges, universities, and students manage and verify academic documents securely and instantly. Follow the steps below to understand how the verification ecosystem works.

01

College Registers on AVA

Colleges create official accounts on AVA, verified by the AVA team. This ensures only legitimate institutions issue and validate documents.

02

Student Profile Creation

Colleges add students to the AVA system. A secure digital vault is created for each student where all verified documents will be stored.

03

Document Issuance

Colleges upload marksheets, certificates, and academic records. Each document receives a unique **Document Number (Doc_No)** and **Issued By** tag.

04

Document Validation

When any other college receives this document during admission or verification, they simply **upload it to AVA** ‚Üí AVA checks the Doc_No with the original database ‚Üí Status instantly becomes **Verified** if it matches.
